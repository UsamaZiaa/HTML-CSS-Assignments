Omelette Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs (4 Pieces)
  • Salt (as per taste)
  • Red Chilli (1 tsp)
  • Mozzarella Cheese (half cup)
  • Oregano (1 tsp)
  • Olive Oil (3 tbsp)
  • Onion (half)
  • Green Capsicum (half)
  • Tomato (2 Pieces)
  • Yellow Capsicum (half)
  • Red Capsicum (half)
  • Black Olive (6 Pieces)
  • Mushroom (6 Pieces)

Method

  1. In a bow, add eggs, red chili, mozzarella cheese, salt, oregano and mix it well.
  2. In a cooking pan, add olive oil,onion,green capsicum,yellow capsicum, red capsicum, tomato, black olive, mushroom and cook it for a while.
  3. Now, add egg mixture and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. After that, add mozzarella cheese.
  5. Use green capsicum, red capsicum, mushroom, black olive, tomato for garnishing.

Your tasty Omelette Pizza is ready now.
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Serve: 2 Persons
